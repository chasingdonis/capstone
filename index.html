Smart Building. Connected

Brief Description

  • Knocknock is a neighborhood community app that creates a digital platform for people to interact with each other, also share conversation, tools, and neighborhood resources.
  • For landlord to connect with the tenants, no matter is screening applications, collecting rent, or communicating with tenants about building maintenances.
  • For tenants to be responsive and share with each other about neighborhood events, common area update, food sharing, security tips, and raise crime awareness.

Problem Statement

There’s lack of integrated app for people to interact within the same building or same neighborhood, and many immigrants and minority families face language barriers when it comes down to communication.

This app is to break down those barriers and let people interact with their neighbors freely and openly by creating a respectful digital space that supports people from different background to understand each other; to integrate different digital tools to bring convenience to life; to build a stronger community that minimizes crimes and raises neighborhood awareness and work together to create a better environment to live in.

Multiple languages support will be available through live-time translation in the app. The website also introduces the features of the app and how to make neighborhood communities livable and strong.

Questions, Thoughts toward a Solution

  • Use live-time translation to achieve fast and convenient digital solutions for mobile app users and second langauge speakers.
  • App has different categories focusing on different topic, and users can choose to communicate in private or public, which is to create more organized layout and user friendly interface for people to use.
  • There’s rent payment option and also other tools like screening applications of new tenants for landlords, neighborhood association resources shortcut and etc. Create digital friendly space and make things easier to handle online.
